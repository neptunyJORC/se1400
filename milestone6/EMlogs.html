EMPLOYEE LOGS - XS-CORP

Log #001
First week here. The onboarding process was weird—very clinical. No names, just ID numbers. No windows. They said it's standard procedure for "containment zones."
Log #014
Heard a sound coming from Sector 7 again. No one's assigned there. Security said to "ignore auditory anomalies." I'm starting to feel uneasy.
Log #023
Something is wrong. People I saw last week are gone. Their desks are empty—like they were never here. I found a folder labeled "EXHUMATION: Phase III." I don't think this is a software company...
Log #031
They changed my ID badge without telling me. It now says “Tier 2 Clearance” but I never applied for that. Since then, the lights outside my unit stay red. I don’t know what that means.
Log #045
The vending machine in Hallway C dispensed a small sealed envelope instead of chips. Inside was a note: “Do not trust her.” There’s no one here by that name.
Log #050
There was an "Employee Wellness Broadcast" piped into my office today. Just static for 3 minutes, then a voice whispered, "The walls remember." Then silence.
Log #062
I passed out during my shift. Woke up with dried blood under my nails and a bandage on my neck. The med-bot said “Routine sample extraction.” I never consented to anything.
Log #077
Someone left a journal on my desk. It’s written in my handwriting, but I don’t remember writing it. The last page just says: “You were warned.”
