Our Services at Alright Games

Here’s what we can do (besides crying about our buggy code):

Godot Engine

We use Godot for 2D and 3D projects alike. It's open-source, fast, and flexible. It is NOT pronounced "Go Dot"

Unreal Engine

Need stunning visuals and cutting-edge performance? We use Unreal to bring high-end projects to life.

Blender Design

From modeling and texturing to animation, we use Blender to build every world and asset from scratch.

We Choose Not to Use Unity

Due to recent changes in their business practices, we’ve decided to avoid using Unity in our projects. We prefer open and developer-friendly platforms!
